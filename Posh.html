POSH Policy (Prevention of Sexual Harassment)

1. Introduction

Leaders Boli is committed to providing a safe and respectful environment for all employees, users, and stakeholders. Our POSH Policy aims to prevent and address sexual harassment in the workplace and on our platform. This policy outlines our commitment to ensuring a harassment-free environment and the procedures for addressing any grievances.

2. Policy Statement

Leaders Boli strictly prohibits any form of sexual harassment. We are committed to:

  • Creating a work environment where all individuals are treated with dignity and respect.
  • Ensuring that everyone is free from sexual harassment and abusive behavior.
  • Providing clear procedures for reporting and addressing any instances of harassment.

    • 3. Definition of Sexual Harassment

    Sexual harassment includes any unwelcome sexual advances, comments, or conduct that creates an intimidating, hostile, or offensive environment. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Verbal Harassment: Inappropriate comments, jokes, or propositions.
  • Non-Verbal Harassment: Displaying sexually explicit materials or gestures.
  • Physical Harassment: Unwelcome physical contact or advances.
  • Online Harassment: Sending offensive or inappropriate messages through digital means.

    • 4. Scope

    This policy applies to:

  • All employees, including full-time, part-time, and temporary staff.
  • All users of our platform, including visitors and contributors.
  • Any third parties interacting with Leaders Boli, including partners and vendors.

    • 5. Reporting Procedures

    If you experience or witness any form of sexual harassment, you are encouraged to report it as soon as possible. The reporting procedure is as follows:

    1:Report Internally: Contact your supervisor or the designated POSH Committee member. All complaints will be handled confidentially.

    2:Formal Complaint: Submit a formal written complaint detailing the incident, including dates, times, and any relevant evidence.

    6. Investigation and Action

    Upon receiving a complaint, Leaders Boli will:

    1:Acknowledge Receipt: Confirm receipt of the complaint within 24 hours.

    2:Investigate: Conduct a thorough and impartial investigation into the allegations.

    3:Take Action: Implement appropriate measures based on the findings, which may include disciplinary actions or corrective measures.

    7. Confidentiality

    All complaints and investigations will be handled with the utmost confidentiality. Information will only be shared on a need-to-know basis to ensure privacy and protect all parties involved.

    8. Retaliation

    Leaders Boli strictly prohibits retaliation against any individual who reports harassment or participates in an investigation. Retaliation will be considered a serious violation of this policy and may result in disciplinary action.

    9. Training and Awareness

    Leaders Boli provides regular training and awareness programs to ensure that all employees and users are informed about the POSH Policy and their responsibilities

    10. Review and Update

    This POSH Policy will be reviewed annually and updated as necessary to ensure compliance with applicable laws and regulations. Any changes will be communicated to all employees and users.
